**School Type:** Public High School

## Background

Jordan is a 14-year-old freshman adjusting to the academic and social demands of high school. As a student in a public high school environment, Jordan is navigating increased academic expectations compared to middle school while also balancing extracurricular commitments.

Jordan is social, enjoys spending time with friends, and values being involved in school life—especially athletics.

---

## Daily Schedule

* **7:00 AM** – Wake up
* **9:00 AM – 3:00 PM** – School (multiple academic classes throughout the day)
* **3:00 PM – 5:00 PM** – Basketball practice
* **5:00 PM – 6:00 PM** – Homework (approximately 1 hour total across all classes)
* **10:00 PM** – Bedtime

Jordan typically completes homework immediately after basketball practice, demonstrating a structured routine during regular weeks.

## Academic Profile

* **Performance Level:** Academically average
* **Motivation Level:** Cares strongly about grades
* **Study Habits:** Completes homework consistently; studies more intensely when tests are approaching
* **Stress Triggers:** Multiple tests or large projects scheduled in the same week

Jordan wants to perform well academically and feels internal pressure to maintain solid grades, especially while balancing athletics.

---

## Extracurricular Involvement

* Active member of the school basketball program
* Competitive and team-oriented
* Enjoys the social and athletic aspects of sports

Basketball is a meaningful part of Jordan’s identity and daily routine.

---

## Time Management Behavior

* Tracks homework and responsibilities primarily using memory
* Does not use a planner or dedicated tracking system
* Relies partly on parental reminders
* Generally manages daily homework well
* Becomes overwhelmed when assignments, projects, and tests cluster within the same week

Jordan’s overwhelm is less about daily workload and more about visibility into upcoming academic demands.

---

## Home Environment

* Has a quiet, dedicated place to study
* Receives reminders and encouragement from parents
* No significant competing responsibilities at home

The home environment is stable and supportive.

---

## Personality Traits

* Social and friendly
* Responsible but not highly structured
* Motivated by grades and performance
* Competitive due to sports involvement
* Feels stress when academic demands feel unpredictable

---

## Core Characteristics Summary

Jordan is a motivated but informal planner. While daily routines are consistent, long-term academic planning is weak. The primary emotional tension arises when multiple academic deadlines converge, creating a sense of overwhelm despite having manageable daily workloads.

Jordan values simplicity, clarity, and structure but does not naturally create those systems independently.
